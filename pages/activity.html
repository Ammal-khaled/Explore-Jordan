The Lost City of Petra

Walk through Petra's narrow Siq and into a city carved by the Nabataeans more than two thousand years ago. The experience combines dramatic scenery, archaeology, and some of Jordan's most memorable viewpoints.

Roman Ruins of Jerash

Explore Jerash's colonnaded streets, temples, plazas, and theaters with enough time to appreciate the scale of the ancient city. The site offers one of Jordan's clearest windows into Roman urban life.

Amman Citadel & Museum

Visit the Amman Citadel for sweeping city views and remains from Roman, Byzantine, and early Islamic periods. It is a compact but meaningful introduction to the capital's long history.
